Guitar Club Plug-in

Plug-in. Play. Pleasure. Turn it on and rock out with Guitar Club Plug-in! Learn how to play and play together with other members of our club.
Our professional teachers will help you to learn to play the guitar and having fun with this beautiful instrument.
We work with small classes, with a max of 5 potential professional guitar players. With our program you will learn step by step to be comfotable with the guitar.
At the end of our program, you learned the basics of playing the guitar and you can play some nice songs!
